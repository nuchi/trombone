Trombone Simulator

How to use it

Click and drag in the image above to control the simulated trombone. The horizontal axis controls embouchure. Move to the right for higher pitch, left for lower pitch. The vertical axis controls slide length: move down to retract the slide, up to extend. The diagonal lines indicate resonances, where the embouchure and the slide length sync up to generate a clean tone (the ones in red are the ones that are out of tune when the slide is fully retracted). I've also added vertical dashed lines at Bb (thick) and F (thin) as rough guides. You can lip up and down to bend notes. The fat diagonal line all the way on the left is the pedal tone.

If you're using a device with a keyboard, you can also hold the "f" key for flutter tongue, and hold the "g" key to growl into the trombone.
